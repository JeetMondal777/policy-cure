# PolicyCures – Insurance Archived for Absolute Clarity

## Overview

**PolicyCures** is a modern, editorially-driven insurance portfolio landing page. It transforms fragmented business data into actionable intelligence, offering high-fidelity extraction and rigorous risk analysis for discerning corporate estates. The site is designed for clarity, trust, and premium user experience.

## Features

- **Hero Section:**
	- Bold headline and subheading emphasizing clarity and intelligence in insurance.
	- Prominent call-to-action buttons for consultation and learning more.

- **Navigation Bar:**
	- Sticky top navigation with links to Solutions, Intelligence, Method, and Journal.
	- Sign In and Consult Expert buttons.

- **Search Widget:**
	- Quick search for policy archives or underwriting terms.
	- Category filter buttons (Life, Health, Motor, Property, Liability).

- **Curated Portfolios:**
	- Bento grid layout showcasing insurance tiers:
		- The Starter (Entry Level)
		- Premium Motor (Elite Choice)
		- Legacy Life (Generational)

- **The Methodology:**
	- Editorial layout describing the three-step protocol:
		1. Neural Comparison (AI-driven market analysis)
		2. Expert Audit (Human underwriter review)
		3. Archival Precision (Digitized, permanent records)

- **Global Trust:**
	- Logos of global insurance and financial authorities, emphasizing credibility.

- **Voice of Authority:**
	- Testimonials from industry leaders and executives.

- **Intelligence Journal:**
	- Grid-based blog/article section with critical perspectives on risk and protection.

- **Newsletter CTA:**
	- Subscription form for weekly intelligence briefings.

- **Footer:**
	- Company info, solutions, company links, social/connect links, and legal policies.

## Technologies Used

- **HTML5** for structure
- **Tailwind CSS** (via CDN) for modern, responsive styling
- **Google Fonts:** Manrope and Inter for typography
- **Material Symbols** for icons

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.
2. All assets are loaded via CDN; no build step is required.
3. Customize content, branding, or sections as needed for your organization.

## Customization

- Update colors, fonts, and layout via Tailwind utility classes or the Tailwind config in the `<script id="tailwind-config">` block.
- Replace images and testimonials with your own content.
- Add or modify sections as your business requires.
